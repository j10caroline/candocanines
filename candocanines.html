MEET MARLEY

With him, Amy wasn’t just the girl
with cerebral palsy - instead, she
was the girl with a friendly black lab.
Marley lets her stand upright, which
is a difficult task every day.

MEET BRODY

He was taught to trigger an implant
to shorten seizures. His special
talent improves the life of his
owner, Terry, and allows Terry
to be independent and confident.

MEET OMA

She brings comfort and reassurance
to her deaf owner, Rachel. Oma
alerts Rachel to things she cannot
hear on her own.
